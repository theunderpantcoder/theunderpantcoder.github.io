Resolutions proposed and accepted at Annual General Meetings (AGMs) can cause the Bylaws to be changed.

 

The latest version has been registered at the Land Titles Office, and is a legal document, which all owners in the strata must comply with.

 

Obsolete versions are still available (below). For greater detail of the changes, please see the resolutions and appendices recorded in the AGM minutes.

 

Despite our efforts to update this website in a timely manner, it is not guaranteed. Please be reminded that paper copies of the Bylaws should be obtained from the Cypress Point Office, to ensure you have the latest legally registered version.
