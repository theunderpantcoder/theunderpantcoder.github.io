The Underpant Coder

How It Works

Last Updated 31-Jan-23

The boxers will fight in Automatic mode at startup, you can just watch.

You can fight a match by using the 6 punch keys, tapping any starts a match.

 

You can score a knockdown with a clean punch to the jaw. But this is difficult, due to the blocking effect of the opponents arms and gloves. However, each punch landed counts. After a number of good body and head punches, eventually the boxer will collapse in a knockdown.

 

Test your skill and get your opponent on the floor more times than he gets you!

 

Tapping Auto returns to automatic mode.
