Strata Property Act Government Website

Guide 12 - CRF and Special Levies

 

Definitions

* AGM = Annual General Meeting (Legally required once per year)

** SGM = Special General Meeting (Adhoc, but formal meetings required for special purposes)

Decisions made at these meetings are legally binding for Strata members, by majority vote of the members.

These meetings are held in accordance with the Strata Property Act. BC. Canada.
